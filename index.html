privacy policy for keebd

last updated: february 5, 2026

overview

keebd ("the app") is a custom keyboard and meme management app. your privacy is important, so here's the short version: keebd does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data. everything stays on your device.

data storage

all data — including images, gifs, tags, and metadata — is stored locally on your device using an app group shared container. this allows the main app and the keyboard extension to access your meme library. no data is stored on external servers.

data collection

keebd does not collect any data. specifically:

  • no personal information is collected
  • no usage analytics or tracking of any kind
  • no crash reporting data is sent externally
  • no advertising identifiers or ad networks are used
  • no network requests are made by the app — keebd does not connect to the internet
  • no cookies or similar tracking technologies are used

keyboard extension & full access

keebd includes a custom keyboard extension. ios requires you to enable "allow full access" for the keyboard to function properly. this permission is needed solely to allow the keyboard to copy images and gifs to your clipboard so you can paste them into other apps.

even with full access enabled:

  • the keyboard does not log keystrokes
  • the keyboard does not transmit any data over the network
  • the keyboard does not access any data beyond your locally stored meme library
  • the keyboard does not record or monitor what you type in other apps

third-party services

keebd does not use any third-party services, sdks, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

photos access

keebd requests access to your photo library solely to allow you to import images and gifs into your meme collection. selected media is copied into the app's local storage. the app does not modify, delete, or upload your photos.

children's privacy

keebd does not collect any data from any user, including children under the age of 13.

data deletion

since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. you can delete individual items within the app or use the "delete all" option to remove your entire meme library. uninstalling the app will also remove all stored data.

changes to this policy

if this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this document. since keebd collects no data, material changes are unlikely.
